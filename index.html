About Us

We are the pioneers of this NGO - Feeding lives. Our vision is to provide the most essential need of a living being which is food being at the pinacle of the triangle.We, Alston and Aaron take the initiative to make the world a better place to live.You can save a life by just donating whatever you wish.I am sure that will suffice the need of ours.

Services we provide with the money we recieve:

Feeding the needy

Mission People

We aim to reach out to most of the needy people in our locality and by your help we can reach out to many more and provide them with food and water.

Feeding stray dogs

Mission Dogs

This mission is set aside to feed all the stray dogs with meat and other food to relieve them of the harsh time of starving for food and searching to bins to fill their stomachs.

Feeding Birds

Mission FlyEat

We look forward to feed the pigeons and crows with nutricious grains to suffice their hunger and water to quench their thirst.

Feeding Cats

Mission Cats

We will feed all the cats in our locality and feed them with nutritious meals like fish bones and milk for them to carry on.

What will we do of your money?

As mentioned earlier we will do our best to fulfil the missions mentioned earlier. Apart from that we will not only feed the needy but also provude people and animals with blankets during a wintery night and some place to stay like an old matress made into a small house for dogs and cats etc. Apart from this we will aim to help the needy which includes humans as well as animals in all possibl ways we can.We will not let your money go to waste so be rest assures.
